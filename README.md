## 🌍 Bird Habitats and Behavior

Birds live in many different places around the world.

### 🌲 Forest Birds

Parrots, owls, and woodpeckers live in forests where trees provide shelter and nesting spaces.

### 🌊 Water Birds

Ducks, swans, flamingos, and kingfishers live near ponds, rivers, and lakes.

### 🏙️ City Birds

Sparrows, pigeons, and crows easily adapt to urban environments.

### ❄️ Cold Weather Birds

Snowy owls survive in freezing climates because of their thick feathers.

## ⚡ Amazing Fun Facts About Birds
Hummingbirds can fly backwards

Parrots can repeat words and sounds
